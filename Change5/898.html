Uden starts like a surging wave. The sea spray note shortly gives way to a bright marine breeze. Uden’s distinct contrast between bergamot and coumarin marvellously conjures up the white limestone cliffs of Amalfi in southern Italy. Perpetually lapped by deep blue waters, the warm shores exhale a breath of enduring oceanic fragrance.
