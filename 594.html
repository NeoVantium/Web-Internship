Oud Stars - Al-Khatt

OUD STARS is an extension line consisting of six perfumes based off of the XJ Shooting Stars line. Entirely dedicated to the traditions of Arab perfume making, The OUD STARS collection is created with prestigious distillations of pure Oud from the plantations of Laos, Cambodia and Borneo. The extensive research and limited production of OUD STARS has been developed with the firm intention of creating a special collection faithful to the traditions of Arab perfume making while integrating the luxurious style and creativity of Xerjoff perfumes.

The names of each perfume has been linked to an ancient book called “Travel of Ibn Battuta” written in 1325 ad. Ibn Battuta, also know as the Marco Polo of the Arab world, described the life and culture of many cities from North Africa to the Far East leaving a great written testimony of that era. Zafar, Al-Khatt, Mamluk, Gao, Najaf and Fars are ancient cities names chosen to name the new OUD STARS collection.

Al Khatt is a mysterious and seductive fragrance, able to move its owner in distant lands. Al Khatt meets debauched jasmine and irrepressible sweetness of vanilla. Such voluptuous fragrance is memorably warm and make sure to obey and beckons for a story from somewhere in the tales of "1000 and one Nights."
