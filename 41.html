IN THE NOSE - JUNE '19

With Summer just around the corner you'll likely find yourself moving more towards fresh and uplifting aromas. This months scent offerings provide a more unique take on the fresh theme before we hit the high heat. Stand out from the crowd with our top 3 recommendations this June. 

SERGE LUTENS - L'EAU FROIDE
Crafted to give the wearer a cold, stark feeling by its clever use of Somalian incense, Mint and Ginger. It's one of the most invigorating fragrances we've ever experienced and a clever example of fresh taken to the extreme. A gendre neutral scent, perfect for him or her.
Retail Price - £85 - 100ml
Sample from £3.50

NASOMATTO - CHINA WHITE
We shouldn't have to tell you the aroma this fragrance tries to replicate. Whether it's something you've experienced first hand or not, it's easy to imagine when this stimulating and heady creation hits you. It's still fresh with mass appeal, though not to be abused due to its strong Parfum concentration. 
Retail Price - £110 - 30ml 
Sample from £6

TOM FORD - FOUGÈRE PLATINE
A modern take on a classical theme by TOM FORD, this green, fresh and masculine fragrance conjures imagary of forest walks on shimmering morning dew. A simplistic and invigorating aroma for those who appreciate their aromatics.    
Retail Price - £162 - 50ml 
Sample from £3.50
