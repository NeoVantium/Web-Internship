Giorgio Armani introduces Acqua Di Gio Profumo, the new intensity. Profumo; meaning Perfume in Italian is an intense, long lasting Parfum for Men. Inspired by the fresh scent of the Mediterranean island of Pantelleria. The Acqua fragrances consist of invigorating marine notes which mean you not only smell good but you feel good too. The fresh spray of the sea salt, the cooling breeze against the skin, the warmth of the sun and the heat within the black sand - the spirit of Profumo.

Concentration: Eau de Parfum

Perfumer: Alberto Morillas
