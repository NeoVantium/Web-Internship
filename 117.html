Bottling and Handling

 

The Decanting
Our samples are assembled by hand and we take every precaution necessary to avoid contamination of the perfumes. This includes working in a clean, dust free environment and employing laboratory practices necessary to ensure quality standards are kept high at all times. When measuring fluid amounts we use calibrated lab grade pipettes to ensure you receive the exact amount of fragrance you are paying for. You may say that we operate a fragrance lab of sorts, but we don’t actually create the perfumes themselves.

The Bottles
All of the bottles we use to package our perfumes feature atomiser spray tops. Spraying your fragrance is by far the best way to apply perfume to your skin. We use plastic atomisers for our 1ml to 10ml sizes, and glass bottles for 50ml or greater. As we ship large quantities of samples we have found that using plastic for our smaller sizes is more practical for reducing breakages during transit. 

The Labelling
It kind of goes without saying but we label all fragrance sizes we supply. This is to ensure there is never any confusion as to what you are receiving or using on your skin. The labels we use are high quality and laminated to ensure no smudging or smearing of what is printed on them. Our labelling adds that professional touch to the product while giving you something suitable to use time and time again.

The Packaging
We go to great lengths to prepare and package your items to ensure their protection during transit. In additional to double checking the seals on all atomisers we further protect them by placing each one in a rolled up sealy bag which will contain any spillage should the worst occur. Higher value samples are packaged in cardboard for additional protection. All small orders are dispatched in jiffy bags marked with fragile labels and larger orders and full sized bottles in boxes using bubble wrap or other suitable internal padding.
